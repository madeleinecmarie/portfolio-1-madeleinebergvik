About me,

Fun fact: I’m half swedish and half norwegian. I grew up in a small town in Sweden, but fastly grew out of it and have since 2015 lived in Norway.

My interest for web developing awakened when I was 14, I used to do blog design (which included html and css) for myself and my friends. Words spread qiuckly in the small town, and suddenly I found myself designing for people at school. It took me almost 10 years before I decided what I wanted to do with my life and the decision fell on Front end developing.

I have always loved being creative, I love the whole process from the beginning to finally see the finish product. This is where I find my excitement and motivation. Good design and minimalism is something I've always been interested in, not only when it comes to web designs but design in general. Websites with bad userbility is something that always made me very annoyed and frustrated, so before becoming a developer I used to think about how I could make it better, and this is something I always keep in mind when developing now.

I’m currently a first year student at Noroff in Oslo, I'm starting my second and last year of the Higher Professional Degree in fall 2021. I have since 2015 worked as a barista, where I got leadership and staff training experience. I'm also a seasonal employee at the jeans company Lee.
